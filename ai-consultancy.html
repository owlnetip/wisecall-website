FAQ

Common Questions

Things people ask before working with us. Anything else, just send a quick message.

How long does a typical project take?
It depends on the scope agreed after the free discovery call. Some clients need a focused setup and design consultation before a first build; others need a longer project or monthly retainer. We move fast because we work closely with senior decision makers rather than disappearing for weeks.

Do I need technical knowledge to work with you?
Not at all. Most of our clients are non-technical founders, directors and operators. We translate your business problem into a working AI system and explain everything in plain language. Your job is to know your business; ours is to build the right tools.

What AI tools and platforms do you use?
We work with the best tool for the job: Claude, ChatGPT, custom models, N8N for automation, plus design tools like Figma. We are platform-agnostic; the goal is what works for your business, not pushing one specific vendor.

Do you offer ongoing support after handover?
Yes. Ongoing support is usually handled through a monthly retainer, with an agreed amount of strategy, development, training or project management time each month. For larger work, we can support the project with a fuller design and development team.

Where are you based? Will you travel?
We are UK-based and travel across the UK for onsite engagements. Europe and international work is delivered through private online sessions, which work well for project builds, reviews and team training.

What if my idea is too vague to start?
That is exactly what the free discovery call is for. We help you move from "we should probably do something with AI" to a clearer brief, a sensible first step and an honest view of whether we are the right partner.
