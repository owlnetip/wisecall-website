FAQ

WiseCall vs Voicemail Questions

Is an AI receptionist better than voicemail?

For most UK businesses, yes. An AI receptionist answers immediately, asks questions and captures structured details, while voicemail depends on the caller being willing to leave a message and someone finding time to act on it.

Do people actually leave voicemails?

Far fewer than businesses expect. Most callers who reach voicemail simply hang up and either try again later, call a competitor, or give up. WiseCall answers before that decision has to be made.

Will callers know they are talking to AI?

WiseCall answers in your business name and can be configured to be upfront about being an AI assistant if you want it to be. The priority is a fast, useful answer rather than pretending to be something it is not.

What happens if WiseCall cannot help with a call?

WiseCall follows your business-approved escalation rules — it can offer a callback, take a detailed message, or route urgent calls to a human, so a call that voicemail would have lost still gets a proper next step.
