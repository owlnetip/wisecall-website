The Reception Reality

Busy doesn't mean reachable

The phone rings while reception is checking in a patient, taking a payment and answering a question about parking. By the time anyone reaches it, the caller has gone.

20 to 40% go unanswered

During peak times, a large share of calls to dental practices go unanswered. Lunch hours are worst, with reduced cover and higher call volume colliding at exactly the wrong moment.

New patients don't wait

New patient enquiries are often the first calls abandoned. They rarely leave a voicemail. They simply ring the next practice, and the lifetime value walks out with them.

Gaps and after-hours

Late-notice cancellations are hard to fill when reception is already stretched. And once you close, out-of-hours calls go entirely unanswered, even from patients ready to book.

The result: a practice that is busy, professional and well run, but quietly losing revenue through calls that simply never get answered.

FAQ

Dental questions, answered

Does it book into our practice management software?
WiseCall is designed to fit around leading dental systems including Dentally, Software of Excellence and EXACT. It captures the appointment and patient details and delivers them into your workflow. We confirm exactly how it integrates with your setup during onboarding.
Does the AI give clinical or dental advice?
No. WiseCall handles scheduling, enquiries and information capture only. It never provides clinical advice or diagnosis. Any clinical question is escalated straight to your practice team.
What happens with a dental emergency call?
You define what counts as urgent. Genuine emergencies are routed to your team or on-call contact immediately, following your escalation rules. Routine queries are logged with a full summary so no caller is left without a clear next step.
Can we keep our existing practice number?
Yes. In most cases your existing number can be ported to WiseCall, or we provide a dedicated number for you. There's no phone system to buy: WiseCall routes each call to the right person and takes a message if no one's available. Prefer to use your existing PBX? You can connect that too. We confirm what's possible during onboarding.
Is the demo on this page the real product?
Yes. The demo panel at the top of this page connects you to a live WiseCall voice agent, not a recording. Your production agent is configured with your practice's details: opening hours, services, booking rules and escalation contacts.
