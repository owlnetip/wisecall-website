# Ashleigh Stone tenant maintenance and repair reporting

Ashleigh Stone manages repairs and maintenance for tenants in managed rental properties. Tenants should report faults and maintenance issues online rather than only by phone.

## How to tell tenants on the phone

Do **not** read out the full maintenance website address on a call unless the caller specifically asks for it.

Instead, tell tenants:
- Go to **ashleighstone.co.uk** (say "our website" or "ashleigh stone dot co dot uk" — do not read out "https" or "www")
- Click **"Tenants report a repair"** in the top-right corner of the website

If the caller is on a UK mobile number, offer to text them the direct link to the repair form instead.

## Online repair reporting portal (direct link — for SMS and reference)

**Report a maintenance issue:** https://ashleighstone-maintenance.10ninety.co.uk/

This is Ashleigh Stone's tenant repair reporting system (10ninety). It is linked from the Ashleigh Stone website under "Tenants report a repair" on the lettings pages.

The online form has four steps:
1. **What is the problem?** — choose the main category, then the specific issue, and add a short description.
2. **Add photos** — optional photos of the fault help the team assess urgency.
3. **Your details** — tenant contact and property details.
4. **Confirm and send** — review and submit the report.

After submission, Ashleigh Stone's lettings and maintenance team will arrange any required repairs and contractor access.

## Problem categories tenants can report

- **Kitchen** — appliances, oven, hob, microwave, extractor fan, units, sinks and taps, flooring, other
- **Bathroom & toilet** — basin, bath, shower, toilet, extractor fan, other
- **Heating** — boiler, radiator, hot water tank, other
- **Exterior, windows & garden** — guttering, roofing, chimney, windows, garden, other
- **Walls, floors & ceiling** — walls, flooring, ceiling, other
- **Doors, garages & locks** — doors, garage, locks, keys, other
- **Lighting & electrics** — light fitting, light switches, power sockets, other
- **Other** — anything not covered above

## Urgent repairs and emergencies

For urgent safety issues such as a gas leak, serious flooding, or no heating/hot water in cold weather, tenants should call Ashleigh Stone immediately on **01702 480 666** as well as reporting online where possible.

If there is immediate danger (for example smell of gas), tenants should also follow emergency service guidance such as leaving the property and calling the National Gas Emergency Service on 0800 111 999.

## Who this is for

This repair reporting portal is for **existing tenants** in properties managed by Ashleigh Stone. It is not for buyers, sellers, or new tenancy applications.

- **New tenant applications:** https://ashleigh-stone-tenant-application-form.paperform.co/
- **Buying or selling property:** speak to the sales team or call 01702 480 666
- **Free property valuation:** Ashleigh Stone offers free, no-obligation valuations — call 01702 480 666 or email property@ashleighstone.co.uk
