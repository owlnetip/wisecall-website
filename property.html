The Branch Bottleneck

Busy doesn't mean responsive

Negotiators are on viewings. Valuers are out. The office is juggling portal leads, chain updates and tenant issues. By the time someone answers, the caller is gone.

Vendors don't wait

Vendor and landlord enquiries are time sensitive. People comparing agents rarely wait for a callback. If your line is busy at lunch or after hours, they try the next agency on the list.

Volume crowds out value

Buyers, tenants and managed landlords create a constant stream of inbound calls. Those calls matter, but they can easily crowd out new instruction opportunities when the team is stretched.

Branches drift apart

Sales, lettings and property management all have different priorities. One missed handoff between branches or departments can mean lost instructions and weaker visibility across the business.

The result: an agency that looks busy and professional, but still loses valuations, viewings and instructions through calls that never reach the right person quickly enough.

FAQ

Property questions, answered

Does the AI actually book valuations, or just take messages?
It books. WiseCall captures the property details, qualifies whether it's a sale or lettings enquiry, and books the valuation or callback into your workflow. Where a call genuinely needs a person, it routes or escalates based on your rules.
Can sales, lettings and property management have different handling?
Yes. Each department or branch can follow its own routing, escalation and data-capture rules while the whole business works to one call-handling standard with shared reporting.
What happens with urgent maintenance calls at 2am?
You define what counts as urgent. Genuine emergencies are routed to your on-call contact immediately. Everything else is logged with a full summary and queued for the morning, so tenants always get a proper response without waking your team unnecessarily.
Can we keep our existing branch numbers?
Yes. In most cases your existing numbers can be ported to WiseCall, or we provide dedicated numbers for you. There's no phone system to buy: WiseCall routes each call to the right negotiator and takes a message if no one's available. Prefer to use your existing PBX? You can connect that too. We confirm what's possible during onboarding.
Is the demo on this page the real product?
Yes. The demo panel at the top of this page connects you to a live WiseCall voice agent, not a recording. Your production agent is configured with your agency's details: branches, fees, availability, escalation contacts and booking process.
