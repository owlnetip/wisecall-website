The Intake Bottleneck

Busy doesn't mean responsive

The phone rings while fee earners are in meetings, reception is handling existing clients and someone is racing a deadline. By the time the call is returned, the enquiry has moved on.

New matters are unforgiving

Prospective clients comparing firms want a fast, confident first response. If the call is missed at lunch, after hours or during a busy patch, they rarely wait around.

Volume crowds out value

Existing clients call for updates, document requests, billing queries and urgent escalations. Those matter, but they easily crowd out valuable new intake when the front desk is stretched.

Departments drift apart

For firms with multiple departments or offices, consistent call handling is harder. One missed handoff can mean lost instructions, slower response times and less visibility into demand.

The result: a firm that looks busy and professional, but still loses opportunities through calls that never reach the right person quickly enough.

FAQ

Legal questions, answered

Does the AI give legal advice?
No. WiseCall captures information, qualifies enquiries and routes calls only. It never provides legal, financial or professional advice. Anything requiring advice is escalated straight to your team.
Does it push intake into Clio?
Yes. WiseCall is designed to push structured intake data into Clio Manage and Clio Grow, as well as custom CRM and shared-inbox workflows. We confirm the exact setup for your firm during onboarding.
How are urgent or time-sensitive matters handled?
You define what counts as urgent. WiseCall identifies time-sensitive matters early and escalates them using your rules, to an on-call solicitor, a duty team or a specific office. Everything else is logged with a full summary.
Can we keep our existing firm number?
Yes. In most cases your existing number can be ported to WiseCall, and every plan includes a complete phone system with extensions and mobile apps for your team. We confirm what's possible for your number during onboarding.
Is the demo on this page the real product?
Yes. The orb at the top of this page calls a live WiseCall voice agent, not a recording. Your production agent is trained on your firm's details: departments, intake questions, routing rules and escalation contacts.
