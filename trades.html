The On-Site Reality

Busy doesn't mean reachable

The phone rings while you are on a job, under a sink, up a ladder or dealing with a customer in front of you. By the time you are free, the lead has often gone elsewhere.

New jobs don't wait

Homeowners and commercial clients usually call more than one contractor when they need help. If your line goes unanswered, many will simply move on to the next business in the search results.

Voicemail loses the lead

Most callers hang up before the voicemail tone, and fewer still leave a message. By the time you call back, the urgency is gone or someone else has already won the job.

Evenings are your peak hours

Customers are at home in the evenings and at weekends, exactly when they have time to ring a tradesperson. Those calls go unanswered and turn into revenue for whoever picks up first.

The result: a business doing great work, but quietly losing income through calls that simply never get answered at the right moment.

FAQ

Trades questions, answered

What happens if I'm on a job when someone calls?
WiseCall answers the call in your business name, captures the caller's details and reason for calling, and books a callback slot if appropriate. You receive a structured summary so you can follow up with full context, even if the call came in while you were under a sink or up a ladder.
Can WiseCall handle emergency or urgent callout requests?
Yes. You define what counts as urgent for your trade. WiseCall can identify emergency or priority calls based on what the caller says and route them to your on-call number immediately, while logging routine enquiries for a normal callback.
Does WiseCall give quotes or technical advice on calls?
No. WiseCall captures job details and routes calls. Pricing, technical advice and emergency instructions always remain with your team. The AI handles the front end, gathering the information you need so you can make the right decisions when you call back.
Can we use our existing business number?
In most cases, yes. Your existing number can be ported to WiseCall, or we provide a dedicated number for you. There is no phone system to buy or contract to sign. We confirm what is possible for your setup during onboarding.
Is the demo on this page the real product?
Yes. Calling the number at the top of this page connects you to a live WiseCall voice agent, not a recording. Your production agent is configured with your business details: services, coverage areas, booking rules and escalation contacts.
