Internal sales document. Last updated: July 2026 (rev. 2 — non-IT / non-comms priority).

**Strategy shift:** Deprioritise VoIP wholesalers and IT/MSP (they already sell comms — WiseCall becomes “addon #7”). **Lead with partners who create or advise on demand but do not sell phone systems.**

**Primary contact:** info@wisecall.io · **Partner page:** https://wisecall.io/partners/

## Partner strategy at a glance

```text
Phase 1 (now)     → Marketers, accountants, HR advisors        [REFER]
Phase 2           → Sector coaches, job software, recruiters      [REFER]
Phase 3           → Associations, franchises, insurance brokers   [REFER / BENEFIT]
Phase 4 (later)   → Regional voice MSPs (refer-only)              [REFER]
Phase 5 (scale)   → VoIP wholesalers when you have case studies   [RESELL]
```

## Partner models

| Model | Best for | Partner does | WiseCall does | Economics |
|-------|----------|--------------|---------------|-----------|
| **Refer** | Marketers, accountants, coaches | Warm intro | Demo, onboarding, billing, support | **15–25% lifetime** on cleared revenue |
