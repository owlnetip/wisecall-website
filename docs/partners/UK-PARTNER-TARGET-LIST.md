# WiseCall UK Partner Target List

Internal sales document. Last updated: July 2026.

Use this list to recruit channel partners using the same archetypes that drove Fonio’s growth (VoIP resellers, vertical IT, MSPs, AI agencies) — with a UK-native, omnichannel pitch.

---

## How to use this list

1. **Start with Tier 1** — highest customer density per partner conversation.
2. **Lead with vertical kits** — dental, trades, legal, property — not generic “AI”.
3. **Offer two models** — Refer (low touch) or Resell (partner owns relationship).
4. **Differentiate vs Fonio** — UK infrastructure, voice + WhatsApp + SMS + email today, Dentally integration, PBX connect.

**Primary contact:** info@wisecall.io (Partner Application)  
**Partner page:** https://wisecall.io/partners/

---

## Partner models (recommended)

| Model | Partner does | WiseCall does | Economics (recommended) |
|-------|--------------|---------------|-------------------------|
| **Refer** | Introduces qualified SME leads | Demo, onboarding, billing, L2 support | **15–25% lifetime** commission on cleared subscription revenue |
| **Resell** | Bills customer, L1 support, owns relationship | Product, platform, L2 escalation | **20–30% wholesale discount** + partner sets retail price |
| **Implementation** | Setup, scripting, integration (all models) | Platform access, partner portal | **Partner bills separately** — typical £500–£2,500 setup |

Partners can mix refer and resell per customer (same as Fonio).

Do **not** lead with “switch from Fonio” in cold outreach. Use these when a partner or prospect already uses or evaluates them:

| Situation | WiseCall message |

| Voice-only today | “WhatsApp, SMS and email are live — one inbox, one summary workflow.” |
