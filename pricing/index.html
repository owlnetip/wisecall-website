FAQ

Pricing questions

What counts as an AI call?

An AI call is a completed inbound call handled by your WiseCall AI receptionist.

What happens if I go over my monthly call allowance?

You can continue receiving calls. Additional AI calls are billed monthly at the rate shown on your plan.

Are features limited on the Starter plan?

No. All plans include the core WiseCall features. The only difference is the number of AI calls included each month.

Can I upgrade later?

Yes. You can move to a higher plan as your call volume grows.

Do you support integrations?

Yes. WiseCall can connect with CRMs, calendars, forms, email, SMS, WhatsApp and other business systems.

What is the Email Channel add-on?

The Email Channel add-on lets your WiseCall AI receptionist handle inbound emails as well as calls. It reads incoming emails, understands the enquiry and sends a reply using your agent's existing knowledge base — the same way it handles calls. It costs £79/month and includes 100 AI email replies. Additional replies are charged at £0.75 each. It can be added to any plan.
