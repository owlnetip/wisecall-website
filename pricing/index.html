FAQ

Pricing Questions

How does WiseCall pricing work?

WiseCall pricing is based on the plan you choose (Starter, Professional or Business) and the number of AI-handled calls, emails, WhatsApp messages, live chat conversations and SMS notifications included each month. The best plan depends on your enquiry volume.

What is included in every plan?

Every plan includes a 24/7 AI receptionist, call summaries and transcripts, appointment booking, call transfers and routing, a dashboard, and a 7-day pilot before the 12-month term begins.

What happens if we receive more AI calls than our plan includes?

If your business regularly exceeds its monthly allowance, we will recommend moving to a more suitable plan. Book a demo and we can advise based on your call volume.
