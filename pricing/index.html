FAQ

Pricing Questions

How does WiseCall pricing work?

WiseCall pricing is based on the plan you choose, the number of AI-handled inbound calls included and the phone system requirements for your team. The best plan depends on your call volume and routing needs.

Does the phone system cost extra?

WiseCall plans include the AI receptionist and a complete business phone system foundation, so teams do not need to buy a separate basic phone system just to start.

What happens if we receive more AI calls than our plan includes?

If your business receives more AI-handled inbound calls than your monthly allowance, additional call handling can be charged as overage or moved to a more suitable plan.
