FAQ

Pricing questions

What's included in every plan?

Every WiseCall plan is a single AI communications platform. AI voice, email, WhatsApp, live chat and SMS are all included, each with its own monthly usage allowance. The plans differ only in how much of each channel you get.

Do I need to buy email, WhatsApp or live chat separately?

No. AI email, WhatsApp, live chat and SMS are built into every plan with their own monthly allowances. There is no separate product or add-on to buy.

What happens if I exceed my allowance?

For one-off busy months you can purchase an AI Communication Pack. If your business regularly exceeds your allowance we recommend upgrading to the next plan.

What counts as an AI call?

An AI call is a completed inbound call handled by your WiseCall AI receptionist. AI email replies, WhatsApp conversations, live chat conversations and SMS each count against their own monthly allowance.

Are features limited on the Starter plan?

No. Every plan has the same features and all five channels. The only difference is the monthly usage allowance for each channel.

Can I upgrade later?

Yes. You can move to a higher plan, or top up with usage packs, as your volume grows.

Do you support integrations?

Yes. WiseCall can connect with CRMs, calendars, forms, email, SMS, WhatsApp and other business systems.
